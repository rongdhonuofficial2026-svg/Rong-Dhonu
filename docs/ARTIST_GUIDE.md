## Creating Your Account

### If you have received an invitation email:

1. Click the **Accept Invitation** link in the email from `noreply@mail.rongdhonu.com`.
2. You will be taken to the Rongdhonu website to set a password.
3. Enter a strong password (at least 8 characters, mix of letters and numbers) and click **Set Password**.
4. You will be logged in and taken to your dashboard.

### If you are registering independently:

1. Visit `https://rongdhonu.vercel.app/register`.
2. Enter your email address and choose a password.
3. Click **Create Account**.
4. Check your inbox for a confirmation email and click the **Confirm Email** link.
5. Log in at `https://rongdhonu.vercel.app/login`.

> Your account starts with the `member` role. To be recognised as a participating artist in an exhibition, an administrator must link your profile to that exhibition. Contact the exhibition committee after creating your account.

## Completing Your Profile

A complete profile helps the committee and the public learn about you as an artist. Your profile appears on the **Artists** page of the Rongdhonu website.

Navigate to your dashboard and click **Edit Profile** (`/dashboard/profile`).

### Bilingual Profile Information

The Rongdhonu platform is bilingual. Please fill in both English and Bengali fields wherever possible so that all visitors can learn about you.

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Full Name (English)** | Your name as it appears in English publications |
| **Full Name (Bengali)** | আপনার নাম বাংলায় |
| **Biography (English)** | A short artist statement or biography (up to 1,000 characters) |
| **Biography (Bengali)** | শিল্পীর পরিচিতি বাংলায় (সর্বোচ্চ ১০০০ অক্ষর) |

**Tips for a strong biography:**
- Mention your artistic style, influences, and training.
- You can list exhibitions you have participated in.
- Write in the first person ("I am a painter based in Dhaka…").
- If writing in Bengali is difficult, the English biography alone is acceptable — the committee may assist with Bengali translation upon request.

### Uploading a Profile Photo

1. On the **Edit Profile** page, click the circular profile image area or the **Upload Photo** button.
2. Select a JPG or PNG file from your device.
3. **Requirements:**
   - Minimum size: 400×400 pixels
   - Maximum file size: 2 MB
   - Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
   - The photo should clearly show your face (headshot style works best).
4. Crop and position the image using the cropper tool.
5. Click **Save Photo**.

Your profile photo appears on the public Artists directory and in your dashboard header.

## Submitting an Artwork

To submit an artwork for consideration in the current exhibition:

1. Click **Submit Artwork** in the sidebar, or navigate to `/dashboard/artworks/new`.
2. Follow the steps below.

### Artwork Image Requirements

The image you upload is what the committee will use to evaluate your artwork, and what appears on the website if approved. Please ensure:

| Requirement | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Minimum resolution** | 2000px on the longest side |
| **Maximum file size** | 10 MB |
| **Accepted formats** | JPG, PNG, WebP |
| **Colour space** | sRGB (avoid CMYK — it will look washed out on screen) |
| **Content** | The artwork itself, clearly lit, without a frame unless the frame is part of the work |

**Photography tips:**
- Photograph in daylight near a window, or with a flash positioned at an angle to avoid glare.
- Use a plain, neutral background (white or light grey).
- Make sure the entire artwork fits within the frame with a small border around the edges.
- Do not apply Instagram filters or heavy edits — the committee needs to see the true colours.

### Filling in Artwork Details

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Title (English)** | ✅ | The artwork's title in English |
| **Title (Bengali)** | Optional | শিল্পকর্মের নাম বাংলায় |
| **Medium (English)** | Optional | E.g., "Watercolour on paper", "Acrylic on canvas" |
| **Medium (Bengali)** | Optional | মাধ্যম বাংলায় (যেমন, "তেলরঙ", "জলরঙ") |
| **Price (BDT)** | Optional | Selling price in Bangladeshi Taka. Leave blank if the work is not for sale. |
| **Exhibition** | ✅ | Select the exhibition this artwork is submitted for. Only active/upcoming exhibitions are listed. |
| **Artwork Image** | ✅ | Upload the image file (see requirements above). |

After filling in all details, click **Submit for Review**.

### After Submission

After submitting:

1. You will see a confirmation message: *"Your artwork has been submitted and is awaiting review."*
2. The artwork appears in your **My Artworks** list with the status **Pending**.
3. The exhibition committee will review it and make a decision.
4. You will receive a notification (in-app and/or by email, depending on your preferences) when a decision is made.

**Review timelines:** The committee aims to review submissions within **7 working days** after the submission deadline. You will be notified either way — please do not contact the committee to ask about status before this period has passed.

## Tracking Artwork Status

You can check the status of all your artworks at any time by visiting **My Artworks** (`/dashboard/artworks`).

### Pending

🟡 **Pending** — Your artwork has been received and is in the review queue. No action is needed from you.

### Approved

✅ **Approved** — Your artwork has been accepted for the exhibition! It will appear on the public website at `/gallery` and in the exhibition catalog.

What to do next:
- Check the exhibition details at `/exhibitions` for hanging/delivery instructions.
- Ensure your biography is complete, as it will be printed in the catalog.
- If your artwork is for sale, verify the price is correctly listed.

### Rejected

❌ **Rejected** — Your artwork was not accepted for this submission cycle.

- Click on the artwork to read the **rejection reason** left by the committee. This feedback is specific and actionable.
- You can edit and resubmit the artwork, or submit a different artwork (see below).

## Editing or Resubmitting an Artwork

### Editing a Pending Artwork

While an artwork is `pending`, you can edit the **title**, **medium**, and **price**. You **cannot** replace the main image of a pending artwork (to prevent changes mid-review). To replace the image, withdraw the submission and resubmit.

1. Go to **My Artworks**.
2. Click the artwork card.
3. Click **Edit**.
4. Update the desired fields and click **Save Changes**.

### Resubmitting a Rejected Artwork

If an artwork was rejected, you can act on the committee's feedback and resubmit:

1. Go to **My Artworks**.
2. Click the rejected artwork card.
3. Read the rejection reason carefully.
4. Click **Resubmit**.
5. You can replace the image (required if the issue was with image quality) and update any other details.
6. Click **Submit for Review**.

The artwork status resets to `pending` and re-enters the moderation queue.

> **Note:** Submission deadlines apply. Resubmissions after the exhibition's submission deadline close date will not be reviewed for the current exhibition cycle. Check the exhibition announcement for deadline dates.

## Notification Preferences

You can control how Rongdhonu communicates with you. Navigate to **Edit Profile** (`/dashboard/profile`) and scroll to the **Notification Preferences** section.

| Preference | Description | Default |
|---|---|---|
| **Email Notifications** | Receive emails for important events | ✅ On |
| **In-App Notifications** | See notifications in the dashboard bell icon | ✅ On |
| **Artwork Updates** | Emails when your artwork is approved or rejected | ✅ On |

**Recommendation:** Keep all notifications enabled during active exhibition periods. Approval and rejection emails often contain time-sensitive information about delivery deadlines.

To update:
1. Toggle the desired switches.
2. Click **Save Preferences**.

---

## Reading Your Notifications

Click the 🔔 bell icon in the dashboard header, or navigate to `/dashboard/notifications`.

The notifications page shows all notifications, newest first. Unread notifications are highlighted. Click any notification to mark it as read and follow the link to the related content (e.g., the artwork that was approved, or the new catalog that was published).

To mark all notifications as read at once, click **Mark all as read**.

## Downloading Exhibition Catalogs

The Rongdhonu exhibition catalogs are free to download for all visitors — no login required.

1. Navigate to `https://rongdhonu.vercel.app/catalogs`.
2. Browse the available catalogs. They are sorted by exhibition year, newest first.
3. Click **Download PDF** on the catalog you want.
4. The PDF will download directly to your device. Each catalog is typically 10–30 MB.

> As an artist, you will receive an in-app notification and email when a new catalog is published. The email contains a direct download link.

If you participated in an exhibition, look for your name, biography, and approved artworks in the corresponding year's catalog.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I submitted an artwork but the deadline has passed. Can I still be considered?**

A: Submissions after the deadline are at the committee's discretion. Contact the committee via email at `committee@rongdhonu.com` explaining your situation. Late submissions are not guaranteed to be reviewed.

---

**Q: Can I submit more than one artwork?**

A: Yes. There is no limit on submissions per artist per exhibition. However, the committee may approve only a subset of your submissions based on available space and curatorial decisions.

---

**Q: My artwork was approved. When and where do I deliver it?**

A: Delivery instructions (date, time, venue, contact person) will be sent to you by email after approval. Check the exhibition page at `/exhibitions` for general venue information.

---

**Q: I cannot log in — I've forgotten my password.**

A: Visit `https://rongdhonu.vercel.app/login` and click **Forgot password?**. Enter your email address and you will receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

---

**Q: My name in the artists directory is wrong.**

A: Update your **Full Name** in the Edit Profile page. Changes appear on the public directory immediately. If you need the catalog corrected after it has been published, contact the committee — a revised version may be issued.

---

**Q: Can I download the catalog in Bengali?**

A: Catalogs are published in the language(s) indicated on the catalog card (`English`, `Bengali`, or `Both`). If a Bengali version of a catalog is available, it will appear as a separate download option on the catalog listing.

---

**Q: I changed my email address. Will my account still work?**

A: Email changes are handled through Supabase Auth. Contact the committee at `committee@rongdhonu.com` to request an email address update, as this cannot be done from the dashboard currently.
